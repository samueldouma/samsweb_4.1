Epidemic Ferment is a hybrid research-publication / experimental magazine / enthnographic manifesto which emerged from my Senior Integrated Project at Kalamazoo College. It investigates the social, psychological, and neurological effects of life within algorithmic media systems. Through a mix of essay, theory, and 'field documentation' (a.k.a. scrolling on Instagram Reels), the project examines how digital infrastructures shape perception, memory, and collective behavior, and how these forces have come to define modern digital life.
